# Getting Started

Welcome! Your administrator has given you access to one or more TV channel **playlists**. This short
guide explains what you can see and do.

## What you have access to

Your account is a **standard user**, so your whole experience lives on the **Dashboard**. You don't
manage sources or settings — instead you get:

- A list of the **channels** you've been granted.
- Ready-to-use **integration URLs** — links you paste into an IPTV player (or open in a browser) to
  watch your channels.
- A built-in **preview player** so you can check a channel right here, no external app required.

## How to start watching

1. Open the **Dashboard** (it's where you land after logging in).
2. Find the **Integration URLs** card. Each playlist you can access has its own **M3U** link (the
   channel list) and **guide** link (the program data).
3. **Copy the M3U URL** and paste it into your IPTV player of choice. The player loads your channel
   list automatically.
4. To check a channel without leaving the app, click it in the **Available Channels** list — it plays
   in the **preview** pane beside the list.

## About your access token

Your streams are protected by a personal **stream token** that's already baked into your integration
URLs — so the playlist **downloads without a login**, but the **streams only play for you**. If you
ever need to invalidate the old links (for example you shared them by accident), use **Regenerate** on
the Dashboard. Old URLs stop working immediately and you copy the new ones.

> **Note:** If a channel you expect is missing, your administrator controls which playlists you can
> see — reach out to them to request access.
