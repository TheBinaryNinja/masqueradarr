# Dashboard (Your Channels)

As a standard user, the Dashboard is your home base for everything you can do: see your channels, grab
your playlist links, and preview a stream.

## What's on screen

- **Welcome card** — your username, your role, and your **stream token** status with a **Regenerate**
  button.
- **Integration URLs card** — for each playlist you've been granted, the published **M3U** link (your
  channel list) and **guide** link (program data). These are your copy-and-paste links for any IPTV
  player.
- **Available Channels** (left) — the list of channels you can watch.
- **Channel Stream Preview** (right) — a built-in player. Pick a channel on the left and it plays here.

## What drives the context you see

Your channel list and integration URLs reflect exactly the playlists your administrator assigned to
you — no more, no less. If the lists are empty, you haven't been granted access to any playlists yet.

## Key controls and what they do

- **Copy** on an integration URL — copies that link to your clipboard, ready to paste into a player.
- **Regenerate** (on the Welcome card) — issues a brand-new stream token. Useful if you shared a link
  by mistake. **Heads up:** this immediately invalidates your old URLs — anything already using them
  stops working until you paste the new links.
- Clicking a channel in **Available Channels** — loads it into the **preview** player on the right.

## How to watch your channels

1. In **Integration URLs**, click **Copy** on the playlist's M3U link.
2. Open your IPTV player and add a new playlist, pasting the URL.
3. (Optional) Also add the **guide** URL in your player so you get program names and schedules.
4. To spot-check a channel without an external player, click it in **Available Channels** and watch it
   in the preview pane.

> **Privacy:** Your playlist file downloads without a login, but the actual video **only streams for
> your token** — so the links are safe to load into your own player, but don't post them publicly.
